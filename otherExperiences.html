Other Experiences

Many of my various hobbies and miscelaneous activities are listed on this page. I'm not amazingly gifted at anything on this page, but I've learned a lot about a wide range of things.

Untitled Manga

G-pen and Micron-pen on Paper

I had the amazing opportunity to take an introductory drawing course at University of Illinois being taught by a graduate student with an interest in Manga (Japanese Comics) Production.

During the class I learned a great deal about broad topics like visual story telling and general drawing techniques, as well as more specific things like Manga pannel design and traditional manga story structures.

Photoshop
