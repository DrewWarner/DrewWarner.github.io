A Degree in the Real

I graduated in December of 2022 with a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science (with a focus on media), and a minor in Linguistics. I’ve taken courses in languages such as Java, C++, Ocaml, and Python and I’ve learned about everything from computer architecture to high level data structures and algorithms. I’ve also taken courses in many sub-disciplines including natural language processing, bioinformatics, computer graphics, and game development (using both Unity and Unreal).

A Passion for the Creative

While my degree is in computer science, I’ve always had a passion for the arts, and I’ve explored this through a wide range of hobbies and more “niche” elective courses. This led me to combine my passion with my knowledge of programming and pursue a number of projects related to game design and human-computer interaction.
